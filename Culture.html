Rich Cultural Heritage

Karnataka's culture is a vibrant tapestry woven with traditions, art forms, and festivals that date back centuries. The state is home to diverse communities, each contributing to its unique cultural landscape.

From the classical dance form of Bharatanatyam to the folk dances like Dollu Kunitha, Karnataka offers a rich cultural experience. The state is also known for its traditional silk sarees, intricate woodwork, and delicious cuisine.

Discover Karnataka

Karnataka is a state in southwest India with Arabian Sea coastlines. Capital Bengaluru is a high-tech hub known for shopping and nightlife. To the southwest, Mysore is home to lavish temples including Mysore Palace, former seat of the region’s maharajas. Hampi, once the medieval Vijayanagara empire’s capital, contains ruins of Hindu temples, elephant stables and a stone chariot.
