Popular Destinations

Discover the most enchanting places in Karnataka

Hampi

Hampi, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, is an ancient village with ruins of the Vijayanagara Empire. Famous for its majestic temples, royal complexes, and the iconic stone chariot.

Coorg

Coorg, officially known as Kodagu, is famous for its coffee plantations, steep hills, countless streams, rich flora & fauna, and breathtaking views. Often called the 'Scotland of India'.

Mysuru

Mysuru, known as the 'City of Palaces', is famous for its rich heritage, magnificent palaces, silk sarees, yoga, and sandalwood. The Mysuru Palace and Dasara festival are major attractions.

Chikmagalur

Chikmagalur, the 'Coffee Land of Karnataka', is famous for its coffee estates, serene hills, and trekking trails. Mullayanagiri, the highest peak in Karnataka, is located here.

Gokarna

Gokarna is a small temple town known for its pristine beaches and religious significance. It's a popular destination for those seeking a peaceful beach holiday away from the crowds.

Discover Karnataka

Karnataka is a state in southwest India with Arabian Sea coastlines. Capital Bengaluru is a high-tech hub known for shopping and nightlife. To the southwest, Mysore is home to lavish temples including Mysore Palace, former seat of the region’s maharajas. Hampi, once the medieval Vijayanagara empire’s capital, contains ruins of Hindu temples, elephant stables and a stone chariot.
